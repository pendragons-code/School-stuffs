Creating and increasing an allowance:
	Impairment for Trade Receivables (Expense)
		Allowance for impairment of trade receivales (contra-asset)

Decreasing the allowance (without writing off):
	Allowance for impariment of trade receivales (contra-asset)
		Reversal of impairment of trade receivables (expense)
	
	Note: reversal is recorded in less other expense with brackets

Writing off debt:
	Allowance of impairment of trade receivables
		trade receivables
