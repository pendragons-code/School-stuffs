Bank loan: recorded in FS as Long Term borrowings: Is the sum of money borrowed and is recorded n the FS as a NCL. It lasts more than 1 financial year which is set by the bank when making the loan.

Bank overdraft: withdrawal from bank exceeds the amount available in the bank and is recorded in the FS as a current liability. The overdraft is also settled as a long as a deposit equal or more than the overraft is made.


A liability is something a person or company owes, usually a sum of money. Liabilities are settled over time through the transfer of economic benefits including money, goods, or services.
