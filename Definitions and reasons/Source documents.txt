Receipt: Acknowledges payment received from the customers immediatey after the business has sold goods or provided services on credit.

Invoice:  Informs credit customer of the amount owed after the business sold goods/provided services on credit.

Remmitance Advice: Informs credit supplier that payment for a specific invoice by cheque has been made.

Credit note: Reduces the amount owed by credit customers who were previously overcharged or when goods have been returned.

Debit note: Incrases the amount owed by credit customers who were previously undercharged.

Payment voucher: Processes payment to credit suppliers. (must be approved by authorized personnel and must be supported by original supplier's invoice.)
