non-metals --> + O₂ to become a non-metal oxide
	--> Neutral --> H₂O
	--> Acidic  --> + H₂O --> acids

metals --> + O₂
	--> amphoteric
		--> Lead oxide, zinc oxide and aluminium oxides (Al₂O₃)
		--> reacts to both acids and alkali

	--> Basic
		--> insoluble --> Bases
		--> soluble --> + H₂O --> alkali
